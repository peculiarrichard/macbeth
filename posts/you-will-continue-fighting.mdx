Building a strong and lasting relationship requires effort and commitment from both partners. Here are some tips to help you and your partner create a strong and healthy relationship:
1. Communicate openly and honestly. Good communication is the foundation of any successful relationship. Make sure to listen actively and express yourself clearly.
2. Show appreciation and gratitude. Let your partner know that you appreciate them and are grateful for the things they do. A simple thank you or a small gesture of kindness can go a long way.
Practice forgiveness. No one is perfect and everyone makes mistakes. 
3. Practice forgiveness and learn to let go of grudges.
4. Show interest in each other's lives. Take the time to learn about your partner's interests, hobbies, and goals. Showing interest in their life will help to strengthen your connection.
5. Make time for each other. Life can get busy, but it's important to make time for your relationship. Set aside time each week to spend together and make it a priority.
6. Be supportive of each other. Show support for your partner's goals and aspirations. Be there for them during tough times and celebrate their successes.
7. Learn to compromise. Compromise is a key aspect of any healthy relationship. Learn to see things from your partner's perspective and find a solution that works for both of you.
8. Keep the spark alive. Keep the romance alive by planning special date nights, surprise getaways, or even small gestures like leaving love notes for each other.
